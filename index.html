What does M## mean?

Each route below is one way the groups can finish. Inside a route, every node is a single knockout match that team is assumed to win to stay alive; nodes read in order from the Round of 32 up. The gold meeting node is the match where the two teams meet. M## is the match's slot on the 104-game schedule: group games are 1–72, Round of 32 is 73–88, Round of 16 89–96, quarterfinals 97–100, semifinals 101–102, final 104.
